Privacy & Cookies

Consent

EU/EEA visitors: by law, non‑essential cookies require prior consent. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time via the cookie banner or settings link.

Data processors

Where to paste scripts: add your cookie banner (CMP) script in the <head> of each page. Then connect GA4 and Clarity through the CMP so they load only after consent.
