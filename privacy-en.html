Privacy notice (GDPR)

Last updated: 01/01/2026

Important note: this document is a basic information template for the test phase. Full compliance depends on your specific setup (legal entity, country, tools actually used, cookies/analytics, etc.). If in doubt, have this text reviewed by a legal professional.

1) Contact

For privacy-related requests or information: info@travirae.com.

2) Types of data processed

  • Navigation data: technical information such as IP address, user agent, request date/time, referrer URL and security logs. This data may be processed by hosting/CDN systems and technical providers.
  • Account data (Affiliate area/Admin): email, affiliate ID, language preference, security settings (e.g. 2FA) and login/activity logs.
  • Affiliate programme data: clicks, “booking clicks” and statistical data used to attribute commissions.
  • Payout data: information needed to handle payout requests (e.g. amounts, status, method). (If you collect IBAN or tax details, they should be listed explicitly here.)
  • Newsletter: email, language selected at subscription, unsubscribe token, status (subscribed/unsubscribed).
  • Communications: messages sent via email or support contact.

3) Purposes and legal bases

  • Account creation and management (registration, login, forgot password, security): performance of a contract / pre-contractual steps and legitimate interest in keeping the service secure.
  • Affiliate programme management (attribution of clicks/booking clicks, calculation of stats and earnings): performance of a contract and legitimate interest in correct attribution and abuse prevention.
  • Payout requests: contract performance and compliance/anti-fraud checks where applicable.
  • Newsletter: consent (you can unsubscribe at any time).

4) Data sharing

Data may be processed by technical providers (e.g. hosting/CDN), by the database/auth provider (Supabase) and by third-party services embedded on the site (widgets, comparators, maps). Each provider processes data according to its own privacy policy.

5) Data retention

We store personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by law. You can request deletion of your account where applicable.

6) Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, rectify, delete, restrict processing, data portability and to object. To exercise your rights, contact: info@travirae.com.

7) Changes

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
