Travirae Affiliate Programme

Monetise your travel audience: 40% revenue share on the net commissions generated, with tiers up to 60% for partners who deliver consistent results.

Sign up Log in

How it works

  1. Sign up for free and get a unique Affiliate ID and tracked links.
  2. Share Travirae destination pages or the homepage on your website, social channels, newsletter or app.
  3. Earn a share of the net commissions Travirae receives from partners when a user completes a booking after clicking your link.

Note: “net commissions” are the commissions actually paid to Travirae by partners (net of refunds, cancellations, indirect taxes, fraud and payment processing fees).

The tier is recalculated automatically at the end of the month based on valid sales attributed to your ID.

Payments and thresholds

  • Minimum payout: 50 USD.
  • Methods: PayPal or SEPA/SWIFT bank transfer.
  • Currency: USD (automatic conversion if you earn in other currencies).
  • Frequency: on the 15th of the following month once the threshold is reached (excluding holidays).
  • Documents: we may request KYC/anti‑fraud checks and tax details for international compliance.

Attribution and validation

Each click on your link includes your affiliate ID. Sales are attributed to the last valid click within a standard 30‑day window.*

*The attribution window may be adjusted to meet partner requirements; any changes will be communicated in advance.
