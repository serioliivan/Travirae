Cookie Policy

Last updated: 01/01/2026

Important note: this Cookie Policy is a basic information template. Compliance depends on the cookies/tools actually used and configuration choices (e.g. analytics/marketing). If in doubt, have this text reviewed by a legal professional.

1) What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that websites store on a user's device (or read from the device) to run the website, improve the experience or provide functionality.

2) Types of cookies

  • Technical/necessary cookies: required for the website to function (e.g. session/login, preferences).
  • Preference cookies: store user choices (e.g. language).
  • Analytics/statistics cookies: measure website traffic and usage.
  • Marketing/profiling cookies: used for personalised advertising or advanced tracking.

3) Cookies and storage used by Travirae

Travirae mainly uses technical and preference tools required for core functionality:

  • Authentication (affiliate area/admin): tokens/cookies or browser storage may be used to keep the session.
  • Language preference: the selected language may be saved (e.g. via cookies or localStorage) to remember your choice.
  • Affiliate tracking: when a user arrives via an affiliate link (parameter ?ref=), the site may temporarily store that information to correctly attribute clicks/bookings (affiliate programme).

If analytics or marketing cookies are added in the future, Travirae may adopt a consent banner and a preference panel.

4) Third-party cookies

Some parts of the site embed or link to third-party widgets/services (e.g. hotel/flight search and booking, maps and comparison tools). These third parties may set cookies or use similar technologies under their own policies. Travirae does not directly control third-party cookies and tracking.

5) How to manage or delete cookies

You can manage cookies in your browser settings (block, delete, allow only certain cookies). Disabling technical cookies may affect some website features (login, language preferences, etc.).

6) Contact

For questions about this Cookie Policy: info@travirae.com.
