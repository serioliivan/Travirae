Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2025-10-01

This notice explains how Travirae processes personal data under the GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, PIPEDA and other applicable laws. We act as a “controller” for our own services and as a “processor” only when explicitly agreed with partners.

Data we collect

  • Usage and device data: IP (truncated), headers, cookies or local storage IDs, pages viewed, interactions, crash logs, approximate location derived from IP.
  • Account/newsletter data: email, language, preferences, time of subscription and consent.
  • Transaction/affiliate data: when you click through to third‑party partners (e.g., hotel/flight providers), we may receive an ID, click timestamp and conversion summary.
  • Communications: messages you send us and related metadata.

Purposes and legal bases

  • Provide and secure the service (Art. 6(1)(b)/(f) GDPR).
  • Communications and customer support (Art. 6(1)(b)/(f)).
  • Analytics and performance measurement (consent where required, Art. 6(1)(a)).
  • Marketing/affiliation and fraud prevention (consent/legitimate interest, Art. 6(1)(a)/(f)).
  • Compliance with legal obligations and defense of claims (Art. 6(1)(c)/(f)).

Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary: security logs up to 12 months; analytics up to 26 months; marketing until you withdraw consent; contractual/account data as required by law.

Sharing and international transfers

We share data with service providers under data processing agreements. If data is transferred outside your country/region, we use lawful mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions.

Cookies and similar technologies

See our Cookie Policy. Through the consent banner you can manage preferences at any time (Preferences / Analytics / Marketing).

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you can request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and object to processing; you can withdraw consent at any time. EU/UK users can complain to a Supervisory Authority; California users may exercise CCPA/CPRA rights (access, deletion, opt‑out of “sale/share”).

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 16; we do not knowingly collect their data.

Security
